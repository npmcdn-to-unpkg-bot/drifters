Join us on an epic journey to the land of Lamas. We are looking forward to tag you along on one of the best travel indie films to be ever made in India.

Why should you come along?

A unique initiatives for the gypsies and the freaks, the weirdos and the misfits, the rebels out there in open, and the rebels still in hiding, for anyone who wants to act like an adult, and for those who wanna feel like a child - put together by some like spirited oddities who held a different day job, but were too obnoxious to merely exist in it.

Amidst the ocean of managed review sites, and the perpetually-on-sale-marketplaces, the travel blogs updated last decade and the highly-customized-personalized-tour-packages offered by invisible-usual non-existent agents, we are a living-breathing-evolving-community that is based on sharing experiences & adding more life to life.

Are you ready to join us - You are more than ready, if you agree to any of these statements:
1. Life is more than that personalized cubicle
2. You know you are a gypsy/travel junkie/explorer, waiting for your people.
3. You know you need to get out, you know the time is now, you know you belong to the road.

So screw your friends and your partners, who were either too lazy, too zombified or too chickened by the idea of a trip of a lifetime, you trusted their "soon one day" and "next year" way too long, pack your bags, feed the dog, say goodbyes and ride along.

"Not all who wander are lost"
